# 🔐 Legacy Lock - Digital Dead Man's Switch

**Legacy Lock** is a privacy-first, client-side web application designed to solve the "Digital Inheritance" paradox. Built in response to rising global trends in digital legacy management (May 2026).

## 🚀 The Problem
What happens to your digital assets, passwords, and sensitive information if you disappear? Most cloud solutions are either expensive or require you to trust a third party with your most private data.

## ✨ The Solution
Legacy Lock acts as a **Dead Man's Switch** that lives entirely in your browser or on a physical USB drive. 
- **100% Local**: No data ever leaves your device. No servers, no tracking.
- **AES-256 Encryption**: Your "legacy" is encrypted using a Master Password of your choice.
- **Smart Timer**: A customizable countdown (from 30s to 30 days) that resets with every interaction.
- **Offline Vault Export**: Generate a standalone, single-file HTML version of your vault to keep on a secure USB drive for your heirs.

## 🛠️ Built With
- **React 19** & **Vite**
- **Tailwind CSS** (Cyber-Vault aesthetic)
- **Crypto-JS** (Industry-standard AES encryption)
- **Antigravity (Google AI)** for rapid prototyping on Ubuntu.

## 📦 How to use
1. Set your **Master Password**.
2. Write your sensitive information in the Vault.
3. Choose your **Check-in Frequency**.
4. Click **Export Standalone HTML** and save the file to a secure physical location (USB, external drive).
5. Give the Master Password to your trusted emergency contact.
